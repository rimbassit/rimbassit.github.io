About Me

When I began my own journey of self-discovery during psychoanalytical training, I realized that we are far too complex for a single approach. Some people understand their past but struggle to change habits, while others change behavior yet still feel stuck. I noticed that we can thrive in one environment and shrink in another, often limited by patterns, beliefs, or habits that stop us from reaching our potential.

To truly support myself and others, I developed a holistic toolkit through years of training and practice across major psychological and developmental approaches. My work is tailored to each person’s needs and draws on multiple “lenses”:

  • Master NLP Practitioner – Precision Lens: Identify and adjust the mental “software” guiding your behaviors, beliefs, values, and strategies.
  • Psychoanalytical & Systemic Specialist – Depth Lens: Explore the roots of patterns keeping you stuck and the “invisible strings” of family, work, or social systems.
  • Therapeutic Arts Practitioner – Expressive Lens: Use creative and alternative ways to access deeper emotional insights when words are not enough.
  • Professional Coach – Action Lens: Turn insight into practical steps and strategies to overcome obstacles and create change in daily life.

I work with individuals, couples, and groups to recognize patterns, gain clarity, and take actionable steps that help them navigate challenges and make real changes in their lives—all in a safe, culturally sensitive environment.

Services

  • Individual Support: Personalized sessions to explore patterns, gain insight, and implement practical strategies.
  • Couple & Family Guidance: Navigate challenges, improve communication, and strengthen relationships.
  • Group Support & Workshops: Skill-building sessions on resilience, confidence, leadership, and personal development.

My Approach

I combine four lenses—precision (NLP), depth (psychoanalytical & systemic), expression (therapeutic arts), and action (coaching)—to create a flexible, personalized approach. Every plan is tailored to the individual or group, addressing both insight and practical steps for daily life change.
