See. Understand. Unlock.

Using four complementary lenses—NLP for precision, psychoanalytical & systemic insight for depth, therapeutic arts for expression, and coaching for action—I help you identify patterns, understand what drives them, and translate insight into concrete change.

About

My work started with a simple realization: understanding alone is not enough, and action without understanding often leads to repetition. People can know their past and still feel stuck, or change behaviors and still feel disconnected.

Through years of training and practice, I developed an integrative approach that adapts to each individual. The goal is not to apply a single method, but to use the right lens at the right moment.

  • Precision (NLP): Work on beliefs, identity, and internal strategies.
  • Depth (Psychoanalytical & Systemic): Explore underlying patterns and influences.
  • Expression (Therapeutic Arts): Access what cannot always be verbalized.
  • Action (Coaching): Turn insight into structured, practical change.
