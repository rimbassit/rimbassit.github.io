About Me

How it started

When I started my own journey of self-discovery, exploring psychology and meditation, I realized humans are far too complex for a single "school of thought." I understood that we can be blooming into our full potential, but we can also be stuck in the past, unable to see the resources we have. I realized that to truly help myself and others, the work needed to be multi-disciplinary, spanning every individual's needs and structure.

Experience

My experience spans over 15 years, covering clinical, educational, and community settings across Canada, Lebanon, and the Middle East, supporting children, adolescents, and adults through their most complex transitions. I help individuals navigate the unique challenges of displacement and identity, providing a safe, multi-lingual space.

Vision and Toolkit

My practice is built on the belief that true lasting transformation requires looking at your life through many lenses:

  • NLP (Precision): Identifying the "software" that directs your habits.
  • Systemic Therapy (Depth): Uncovering hidden family patterns.
  • Therapeutic Arts (Expression): Accessing insights when words are not enough.
  • Professional Coach (Action): Creating concrete plans for real-world change.
