About Me

How it started

When I started my own journey of self-discovery during my psychoanalytical studies and training, I realized that we are far too complex for a single "school of thought". I understood my past but couldn't change my habits, and saw people who only changed their habits but still felt disconnected and stuck.

I understood that we can be blooming and thriving in one environment, and fading away and shrinking in another, not being able to see the resources we have, nor use them. We are guided by limiting beliefs stopping us from reaching our full potential.

I realized that to truly help myself and others, the work needed to be on many levels and techniques, depending on every individual’s needs and structure. That lead to years of training and practicing across major psychological and developmental pillars to develop my own vision and tool kit.

Experience

My experience spans over 15 years covering clinical, educational, and community settings across Canada, Lebanon, and the Middle East, supporting children, adolescents, and adults through their most complex transitions. I help individuals navigate the unique challenges of displacement and identity.

Whether working through deep-seated patterns or seeking immediate personal growth, my goal is to provide a safe, multilingual, cultural aware space so you can See, Understand, and Unlock your potential.

Vision and Toolkit

My practice is built on the belief that true lasting transformation requires looking at your life through many lenses:

NLP: Precision

My "precision" lens. I use Neuro-Linguistic Programming to help you identify and recode the mental "software" that is pulling you in directions you don't want to go: Your belief system, your values, your identity, and your strategies.

Psychoanalytical & Systemic

These are my "depth" lenses. They allow us to look at the hidden reasons why you might feel stuck or keep repeating the same patterns.

Therapeutic Arts

This is my "expressive" lens. Sometimes, the most important things we need to say don't have words yet. I use simple alternative ways of communication to bypass the "logical mind" and access deeper emotional insights when needed.

Professional Coach

This is my "action" lens. We don't just talk; we create a simple plan so you can start seeing real changes in your daily life immediately.
